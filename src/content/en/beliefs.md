## My beliefs

My first goal is to create good user experiences.

Software has a big influence on our lives, some helps us to become better, some
are harmful. I want to create useful software.

A project is not just a folder of files, the collaboration between all the actors of a project (technical and
non-technical) is essential and creates value.

Investing in the quality of a project takes effort, but significantly increases productivity.

A good development experience increases productivity, encourages innovation and initiatives. I like
automate and facilitate the life of my colleagues using simple tools and documentation, to speed up
projects.
