## My work

**I develop apps**. I am interested in my clients' business, and I create tools to solve
their problems and improve their productivity.

**I develop web interfaces**, I particularly like React and Typescript, and I also use Angular
or VueJS.

**I develop server software**, I like Typescript or Java, and I'm also interested in Scala, Golang
and Rust.

**I deploy my applications or third-party applications**, I particularly like Kubernetes, and I use
also Ansible, SSH, Python and Bash.

**I document** my applications and my work, so that users and developers no longer need to
me when my work is done.

**I'm a fan of free software**, I explore new tools and codes available online every day,
I would like to contribute more regularly.

These days, in my spare time I work on a free mapping software: [Abc-Map](https://abc-map.fr).
Its operation is [explained in video 📹 (5 minutes)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=bXl3Uq5fU34).
